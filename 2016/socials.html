Socials

An important part of any conference is being able to network with other delegates and PHP UK is no exception. We will be hosting three social events - before, during and after the conference.

Wednesday

February 17

Wednesday night we will kick off the conference festivities with a PHP London style pubcon! We'll be meeting at Concrete (located on the lower ground floor of Pizza East restaurant) from 7pm for drinks and pizza while meeting up with other conference attendees and local PHP Londoners. At 8pm special guest Michael Cullum will be presenting his talk "PHP FIG: The PSRs you've never heard of". Drinks and chat will then continue after the presentation.

Getting In

The pre-conference social is open to anyone with an interest in PHP.

Thursday

February 18

Thursday evening we'll be hosting our mid-conference social at the conference venue until 9pm giving you a chance to meet and network with all the other delegates and check out the exhibits too.

Food and Drink

We'll have some free drinks followed by a cash bar, free food including burgers (beef burgers and veggie burgers) and snacks on offer.

Hackathon

If you're participating in the Hackathon or want to pop by and check it out, it'll be running in the Lower Sugar Room until 11pm.

Gaming

There will be a gaming room downstairs in the coffee bar room (James Watt room) with several board games and card games. Feel free to bring a game of your own if you'd like to play it with fellow attendees!

Friday

February 19

Friday night we'll be hosting our post-conference social at the nearby Blueberry Bar for some ping pong, tunes, and cocktails as we wind down the conference. We have exclusive private hire of the ping pong bar upstairs on the second floor from 7pm til midnight.

Getting in

Please bring your conference badge along as you'll need it to get in. Enter through the second door on Paul Street, marked number 4, not the main door of the bar. There will be signs and a doorman to let you know its the PHP UK party.
